About Me

"A portrait is not created in the camera, but on either side of it."

I am an Irish photographer based in County Laois, Ireland. My interests are very varied. I like to shoot anything from weddings to astro. I mainly shoot landscapes, seascapes, nature/wildlife and portraits, but I will take a shot of anything interesting. I hate the term 'Professional Photographer', I'm a photographer - it's as simple as that. They say your first 10,000 photographs are the worst. I have taken so much more than that, so if that makes me a professional photographer - that's fine! Photography has nothing got to do with cameras, in my opinion.

A poet friend said to me "your photos are fantastic, you must have a great camera". I replied "your poems are brilliant, you must have a great typewriter!"

Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on camera is captured forever. It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything. When I'm gone from this earth and my children are looking through my wonderful landscapes, sunrises, sunsets, the photos I hope they keep are the sh**ty blurry ones of their birthdays and moments together - that's where the memories are.
